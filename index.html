About Me

Hello! I’m a writer passionate about short fiction, thoughtful essays, and exploring the small moments that shape our lives.

On this site, you’ll find a collection of my Czech stories, English translations, and a few essays on topics that interest me — from language to technology to everyday philosophy.

Background

I began writing in my teens and never really stopped. By day, I work in tech; by night, I tinker with words. I’m especially drawn to character-driven stories, minimalism, and anything that evokes a quiet sense of wonder.

Interests

  • Short fiction & microfiction
  • Languages (currently learning Finnish)
  • Digital minimalism & web craftsmanship
  • Tea, slow mornings, and quiet notebooks
